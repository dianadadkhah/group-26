Code of Conduct
Our project team is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone. We welcome contributors of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels, and we believe that diversity strengthens the quality and impact of our work.

1. Diversity and Inclusivity Statement
We value and respect the contributions of all individuals, regardless of gender identity, race, ethnicity, disability, age, religion, country of origin, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background, or level of technical experience. Our goal is to foster a learning and working environment where everyone feels supported, heard, and able to participate fully.

2. Expected Behaviour
All participants are expected to:
* Treat all team members and contributors with respect and professionalism
* Provide constructive feedback and be open to receiving feedback
* Communicate clearly and kindly, especially during disagreements
* Use inclusive language and practices
* Collaborate actively, share responsibilities, and support teammates
* Ask questions freely and encourage a positive learning culture
* Respect deadlines, roles, and the team's workflow

3. Unacceptable Behaviour
The following behaviours are not acceptable under any circumstances:
* Harassment, discrimination, or exclusion of any kind
* Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
* Intimidation, bullying, or aggressive communication
* Sharing private information without consent
* Deliberately disrupting discussions, progress, or meetings
* Dismissing or belittling someone’s abilities or background
* Any behaviour that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

4. Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour
If unacceptable behaviour occurs, the team may take any of the following actions:
* A verbal or written warning
* Temporary or permanent removal from team communication channels
* Removal from specific tasks or responsibilities
* Reporting the behaviour to course instructors or academic authorities


5. Reporting and Handling Violations
If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it as soon as possible. Reports may be made to:
* The project lead
* Course instructors or teaching staff
* UBC academic support services
When reporting, please include:
* A description of the incident
* Date, time, and location
* Individuals involved
* Any supporting evidence 
All reports will be handled confidentially, respectfully, and promptly. The safety and well-being of participants will always be the top priority.

6. Commitment
By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help create a collaborative, positive, and respectful environment.
